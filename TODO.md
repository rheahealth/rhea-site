# Rhea Health Landing Page — Prioritized Recommendations

## 1. Add Trust and Social Proof

**Objective:** Establish credibility and reassure users that Rhea Health is a clinically reliable, professional service.

**Actions:**
- Include testimonials or patient stories (anonymized if needed).
- Display institutional affiliations (Duke-NUS, A*STAR, KKH) with logos.
- Link to publications, validation data, or lab accreditation (CAP/CLIA/ISO).
- Add clinician endorsements or quotes from experts.

---

## 2. Disclose Pricing, Logistics, and Turnaround

**Objective:** Increase transparency and reduce hesitation by providing essential practical information.

**Actions:**
- Include a pricing range (“from S$XXX” or “contact us for pricing”).  
- Clarify shipping process and sample collection method.  
- State turnaround time (e.g., “Results within 2–3 weeks”).  
- Mention whether insurance or subsidies apply.  

---

## 3. Reinforce Privacy, Data Protection, and Legal Disclaimers

**Objective:** Build trust by showing clear commitment to confidentiality and regulatory compliance.

**Actions:**
- Add a brief privacy statement on the homepage:  
  *“Your genetic data is encrypted and never shared without consent.”*
- Link to a detailed Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.  
- Include a disclaimer clarifying that results should be discussed with a healthcare provider.  

---

## 4. Strengthen CTA Design and Funnel Clarity

**Objective:** Improve conversion rates by making the next step obvious and persuasive.

**Actions:**
- Use stronger CTAs such as “Book Free Consultation,” “Order Kit,” or “Start Screening.”  
- Make primary CTA visually distinct (color, contrast, placement).  
- Add a simple 3-step visual funnel diagram (Order → Collect → Results + Counseling).  
- Introduce mild urgency (“Limited early access pricing,” “Book before [date]”).  

---

## 5. Expand Content Depth and FAQ

**Objective:** Educate visitors and improve SEO by addressing questions and concerns.

**Actions:**
- Expand FAQ: “What does this test detect?”, “What are false negatives?”, “How accurate is it?”  
- Include an educational “Learn” or “Blog” section with short articles (e.g., “What is Carrier Screening?”).  
- Consider adding a downloadable guide or brochure.  

---

## 6. Visual and UX Enhancements

**Objective:** Improve engagement and clarity through visual design and usability.

**Actions:**
- Add illustrative icons for the “How It Works” steps.  
- Include an anonymized example of a results report.  
- Optimize layout for mobile and tablet users.  
- Improve load speed via image compression and lazy loading.  
- Use imagery that evokes care, family, and confidence.  

---

**Summary Table of Priorities**

| Priority | Focus Area | Expected Impact |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Trust & Social Proof | Builds credibility, lowers hesitation |
| 2 | Transparency (Pricing, Logistics) | Reduces friction and drop-off |
| 3 | Privacy & Legal Reassurance | Increases confidence and compliance |
| 4 | CTA & Funnel Clarity | Drives conversions |
| 5 | Content & FAQ Expansion | Improves SEO and education |
| 6 | Visual & UX | Enhances engagement and readability |
