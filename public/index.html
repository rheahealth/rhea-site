Frequently Asked Questions

What is carrier screening?

Carrier screening checks if you carry genes that could affect your child’s health if both partners carry the same condition.

When should I get screened?

Ideally before pregnancy, to allow time for informed decision-making and options.

How accurate are the results?

Our technology provides high-resolution results with clinical-grade accuracy.
