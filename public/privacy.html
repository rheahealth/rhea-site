Privacy Notice

Rhea Health respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in line with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

What we collect

We may collect your contact details (e.g., name, email, phone) and information relevant to preconception genetic screening.

Required vs optional

- Email: required to process your request and share screening instructions/results.
- First name: optional, used to personalise communications.
- Phone: optional, used only if you request a call-back or for service coordination.
- Marketing email consent: optional — you will still receive transactional messages related to your request without it.

How we use your data

We use your data to: (a) respond to your enquiry; (b) provide screening-related information; (c) arrange and administer requested services; and (d) conduct service improvements and operations. We will not sell your data.

Marketing communications

We only send marketing communications with your consent. You may withdraw consent at any time by following unsubscribe options in our emails or contacting us as described below. We do not send marketing SMS/calls without your express consent and we comply with applicable Do Not Call provisions.

Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes stated or as required by law, after which we will delete or anonymise it.

Access, correction, and withdrawal

You may request access to or correction of your personal data, or withdraw your consent, by contacting us. We will respond in accordance with the PDPA.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and secure our Site. Specifically:

Cloudflare Turnstile — We use Cloudflare Turnstile on our enquiry form to distinguish human visitors from automated bots. Turnstile may set a cookie or use browser signals for this purpose. No personal information is linked to these cookies. For details, see Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Session and Functional Cookies — Our Site may set short-lived session cookies required for basic site functionality. These are deleted when you close your browser.

We do not currently use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies. If this changes, we will update this notice.

Third-Party Service Providers

To operate our services, we share data with the following processors under appropriate data processing agreements:

- Netlify — Website hosting and form-processing infrastructure (United States)
- Supabase — Secure database for enquiry data (United States / AWS regions)
- Cloudflare — Bot protection (Turnstile) and security (Global CDN)
- Laboratory partners — Genetic analysis for clients who proceed to testing (Singapore and accredited overseas labs)

These providers process your data only as necessary to deliver their services and are contractually prohibited from using it for their own purposes.

International Data Transfers

Our website infrastructure and cloud services may involve transferring your personal data outside Singapore. Where this occurs, we take steps to ensure an adequate level of data protection is applied, in accordance with the requirements of the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA). This includes contractual safeguards with our service providers.

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in significant harm to you, we will:

1. Notify the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) as soon as practicable, and in any event within 3 business days of confirming the breach (for notifiable breaches under the PDPA).
2. Notify affected individuals if the breach is likely to result in significant harm.

We maintain internal procedures for assessing, containing, and reporting data security incidents.

Contact

For privacy matters, please contact our Data Protection contact at privacy@myrheahealth.com.
